INSTITUTIONAL FOCUS
-------------------
RepoReserve is designed for institutions that participate in secured
funding markets through custodians and established market infrastructure.

Our goal is to support operational clarity without replacing existing
financial market infrastructure. We work within the structures that
institutions already trust — custodian banks, clearinghouses, and
central counterparties.

We do not disintermediate. We coordinate.

FAQ (×6)
--------
Q1: What does RepoReserve do?
A1: RepoReserve is a treasury collateral visibility and repo operations
    coordination platform. It provides institutional participants with a
    consolidated view of collateral positions across custodians and
    counterparties.

Q2: Who is it designed for?
A2: Treasury teams at institutional banks, custodian banks, and
    clearinghouses that manage repo and collateral operations at scale.

Q3: Is this a trading platform?
A3: No. RepoReserve is an operations coordination and visibility
    platform. It does not execute trades, provide pricing, or act
    as a broker.

Q4: Why is an NDA required?
A4: Product details, architecture, and workflows are proprietary. We
    share these only with vetted institutional counterparties under
    mutual non-disclosure agreements.

Q5: Can I see a demo before signing an NDA?
A5: We provide a general overview and this institutional brochure.
    Detailed demonstrations and documentation are available only after
    executing a mutual NDA.

Q6: How do I get started?
A6: Submit a confidential discussion request. A member of our team will
    respond within two business days to schedule an initial conversation.
