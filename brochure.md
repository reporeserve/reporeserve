# RepoReserve

**Treasury Collateral Visibility & Repo Operations Coordination**

---

## The Challenge

Institutional treasury teams manage collateral across multiple custodians, counterparties, and internal books. Fragmented data creates reconciliation overhead, delays settlement decisions, and obscures risk exposures that compound over time.

## Our Platform

RepoReserve provides a unified operational visibility layer for collateral positioning and repo operations coordination. We serve institutional treasury teams, custodian banks, and clearinghouses — the organizations responsible for the integrity of post-trade infrastructure.

### What We Deliver

- **Consolidated Collateral Visibility** — A single, authoritative view of positions across custodians and counterparties, reducing manual reconciliation and surfacing discrepancies before they escalate.

- **Repo Operations Coordination** — Streamlined lifecycle coordination between treasury desks, custodians, and market infrastructure providers, designed for institutional scale and governance requirements.

- **Risk & Compliance Reporting** — Institutional-grade audit trails and position reporting that support board-level oversight, regulatory compliance, and internal risk management.

## Institutional Focus

RepoReserve is designed for institutions that participate in secured funding markets through custodians and established market infrastructure. Our goal is to support operational clarity without replacing existing financial market infrastructure. We do not disintermediate. We coordinate.

## Who We Serve

- **Custodian Banks** managing collateral on behalf of institutional clients
- **Clearinghouses & CCPs** requiring consolidated position visibility
- **Institutional Treasury Teams** coordinating repo operations across multiple counterparties
