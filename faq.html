Frequently Asked Questions

High-level responses for institutions exploring RepoReserve.

What is RepoReserve?

RepoReserve is a treasury collateral visibility and repo operations coordination platform for institutional participants in repo markets.

Who is it designed for?

RepoReserve is designed for institutional treasury teams, repo market participants, and post-trade operations teams that require structured operational visibility.

How can institutions request access?

Institutions can submit a confidential request through our demo or briefing channel. Our team responds with next steps and engagement scope guidance.

Does RepoReserve replace existing market infrastructure?

No. RepoReserve is a complementary operational coordination layer and does not replace existing market infrastructure, clearing systems, or institutional processes.

Is RepoReserve a trading platform?

No. RepoReserve does not execute trades or provide broker functions. It supports institutional visibility, coordination, and governance requirements at an operational level.
