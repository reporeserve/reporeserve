FAQ

High-level answers for institutions evaluating RepoReserve.

What is RepoReserve?

RepoReserve is an institutional platform category focused on treasury collateral visibility and repo operations coordination across participating organizations.

Who is it designed for?

It is designed for institutional market participants such as treasury teams, funds, custodial entities, and clearing-aligned participants.

How can institutions request access?

Institutions can request an onboarding conversation through the institutional briefing channel to evaluate alignment, use case fit, and participation requirements.

Does RepoReserve replace existing market infrastructure?

No. RepoReserve is positioned as a complementary operational coordination layer that supports existing infrastructure and institutional processes.
